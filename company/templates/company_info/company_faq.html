Returns

What if I want to return a product?

You are always welcome to make a return to us if you do not want to keep the products, as long as it has not been more than 3 months since you ordered the goods. However, we cannot make any exchanges, so if you wish to order another product, you must place a new order!

Return address:

7 Victoria Road
Little Corby
CA4 8BG Bilzen


Important!

  • The product must be in the same condition as when you received it.

  • Remember to send your name and order number in the package, so it will be faster for us to process your return. Also mark which products you have returned.

  • Be aware that it may take some time before the warehouse has received and registered your return. Normally it takes around 3 weeks, but it can also take longer.
    If you still haven't heard from us, don't hesitate to reach out!

  • You as the customer are responsible for the return cost and are responsible for the return until it has arrived at our warehouse, so it is important that you make sure that it is packaged safely and well!



Can I return a product if I regret my purchase?

If a product was not what you expected, you do not need it or you do not wish to keep the product for any reason, you are of course welcome to return it to us. When we receive the return into our warehouse, we will issue a refund on it. The return is only accepted if the product is in new condition and has not been opened or used.



What if my product is damaged?

The right of complaint covers products that are defective or have errors according to current law. If your product arrives broken or with defects, please contact us as soon as you discovered the error. We ask that you contact us by email when you make a complaint because we need pictures or a video of the defective product. Always write the order number in the email so we know which order applies. We are of course responsible for the return shipping in the event of approved complaints.

Order

Can I make changes to my order?

Unfortunately, we are unable to make any changes to your order. This includes i.a. choice of agent, delivery address, payment method and adding or removing products. But if the warehouse hasn't had time to pack your order, we can see if we can cancel it. If you want help with this, you can either contact our customer service, or log into your member account here on the website and cancel your order there.

Check my order confirmation

When you have placed your order, you will receive an order confirmation from us. It can be good to double-check that all information regarding your order is correct, such as personal information and that the correct products have been included in your order. Should you discover an error in the order, please contact us as soon as possible so that we can correct the error.
We cannot guarantee that we will have time to change anything, as our warehouse packs and ships the orders as quickly as they can.



I have received the wrong product.

Contact our Customer Service team with the following information as soon as possible if you discover that you have received an incorrect item in your order:

Order number
Name & product number of the incorrect item
Picture of the faulty item
Which product is it that you are missing

Delivery

If you shop for more than 30$, you get free shipping.

How long is the delivery time?

We usually pack our orders within 48 hours, you can see the current delivery time on the website or in the shopping cart.
Please note that during certain periods the packing time & delivery time may be longer.

Products

If you receive an order of lower quality than expected or if, for example, the color of the yarn does not correspond to the pictures we have on the website, you are welcome to get back to us with pictures of the product you are dissatisfied with & explain what it is that makes you want a new product or money back.

Can I reserve a product?

Unfortunately, you cannot reserve any products with us, here it is first come, first served.
