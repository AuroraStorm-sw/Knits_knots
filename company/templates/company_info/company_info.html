Who are we?



About us

Knits&Knots is a company with a deep-rooted passion for the art of handicraft materials, particularly focusing on yarns
and threads. Founded on the principles of love and dedication, our company has become a household name in the crafting
industry, known for its high-quality products and exceptional customer service.

Our journey began with a simple dream to share our love for handmade creations with the world. We believe that the
process of creating something from scratch using one's own hands is not only extremely fulfilling but also has
therapeutic benefits. With this in mind, we set out to source the finest quality yarns and threads from around the
globe.

What we offer

At Knits&Knots, we take great pride in offering an extensive range of products to cater to all levels of crafters – from
beginners to experts. Our carefully curated selection includes various types of yarn such as cotton, wool, and
acrylic, along with different weights and colors to suit every project. Additionally, we also offer a wide variety of
threads in different materials and shades for embroidery enthusiasts.

Innovation and Quality

Innovation is at the heart of everything we do at Knits&Knots. Our team works tirelessly to constantly enhance our
product line by introducing new textures, blends, and color palettes. We understand that every crafter has their unique
style and preferences; therefore, we strive to provide a diverse range that caters to everyone's individual needs.

Quality is paramount to us at Knits&Knots. We have established strong relationships with our suppliers who share our
commitment towards providing top-notch materials for our customers. Each product goes through a rigorous quality control
process before it reaches your hands so you can trust that you are getting only the best.

Sustainability

Sustainability is an integral part of our business philosophy at Knits&Knots. We actively seek out
environmentally-friendly options for packaging and sourcing raw materials while minimizing waste production throughout
our operations.

Why shop with us?

Our love and dedication to our craft and customers have not gone unnoticed. Over the years, we have received numerous
accolades for our exceptional products and services, including being recognized as a top supplier in the crafting
industry. However, the most rewarding aspect of our work is hearing from our satisfied customers who continue to support
us and inspire us to keep pushing boundaries.

As time goes on...

As we continue on this journey, we remain committed to upholding our values of love, dedication, innovation, quality,
and sustainability. We are grateful for the opportunity to be a part of your crafting experience and look forward to
serving you with our finest materials for many more years to come. Thank you for choosing Knits&Knots – where love meets handicraft.
